Knowledge Lifecycle Framework for quantitative trading with contrastive state discovery and hierarchical expert routing.
Hierarchical architecture with shared encoder and lightweight adapters reduces parameters by 68.7% vs. independent experts.
Bidirectional knowledge flow enables expert-specific knowledge to enrich the shared encoder, improving knowledge gain by 15.5%.
System achieves positive Sharpe ratios on 16/18 stocks (88.9%) with cross-stock Sharpe std of 0.55 and permutation test p = 0.0006.
Zero-shot generalization from A-shares to US equities with only 6.2% accuracy degradation.
